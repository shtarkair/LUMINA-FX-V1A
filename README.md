# Lumina FX V1A — DMX Lighting Controller

**Your stage. Your light. Your rules.**

A professional DMX lighting controller with MIDI support, cue management, and live show features. Built for lighting designers who want full control — no compromises.

---

## Get Started (Mac)

Boom — three steps and you're rocking:

1. Download **Lumina-FX-V1A-Mac.zip** from the [Releases page](https://github.com/shtarkair/LUMINA-FX-V1A/releases)
2. Unzip and double-click **Lumina FX.app**
3. That's it. Go light up the world.

> **Note:** First launch on Mac? If the app won't open, right-click → Open, or run `xattr -cr "Lumina FX.app"` in Terminal. One time only.

## Get Started (Windows)

1. Download **Lumina-FX-V1A-Windows.zip** from the [Releases page](https://github.com/shtarkair/LUMINA-FX-V1A/releases)
2. Unzip and double-click **Lumina FX.vbs**
3. Done. Let there be light.

On first launch, the app will automatically install everything it needs. Sit back, let it do its thing.

---

## What's Under the Hood

- 64 DMX universes x 512 channels — that's 32,768 channels of pure control (ArtNet + sACN/E1.31)
- MIDI controller support — plug in your M-Audio, launch pads, whatever you got
- Cue list with sequences — build your show, hit go
- Live show controls — BPM sync, master fader, effects on the fly
- Fixture library with custom profile builder — your rig, your way
- Show file save/load with USB support — take your show anywhere
- Full Undo/Redo — because mistakes happen
